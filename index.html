Asian Elephant

Asian Elephants are slightly smaller than african savanna elephant. They are thought of have originated from the sub-saharan Africa. In some parts of asia, the are considered sacred and taken as an object of worship.

African Savanna Elephant

African savanna elephants are the largest of the three species of elephants. They are spread around 37 countries in Africa.

African Forest Elephant

African forest elephants are the smallest of the three species of elephants. They are mostly found along the Congo Bain and the west africa. .
